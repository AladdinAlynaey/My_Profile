My Story

My journey into the world of technology began with a strong foundation in mathematics, which I pursued both academically and professionally as a teacher. This mathematical background proved invaluable as I transitioned into the realm of artificial intelligence and data science.

After earning my Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from the International University of Technology Twinteck, I dove headfirst into the exciting world of AI development. My passion lies in creating intelligent systems that can understand, learn, and assist humans in meaningful ways.

Today, as an AI Specialist at DeepSafer, I focus on developing cutting-edge solutions using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems, large language models, and advanced data engineering pipelines. I believe that the future of technology lies in making AI accessible, ethical, and truly helpful for everyone.
